Digital illustration it's popular and essential for many artists. But it wasn't always like this.Digital art emerged in the post-war era. Its pioneers were engineers like A. Michael Noll who used computers in 1962 to create images using algorithms. Digital illustration becomes more accessible through the years and the most widely used techniques are:

  • Vector design
  • Pixel Art
  • Illustration
  • Digital illustration
  • 3D illustration
  • Traditional techniques mix with digital ones

Digital illustration advances with technology and now a world without it is unimaginable
